About Me

Projects

  • Raman Effect: Discovered the inelastic scattering of light, where light changes wavelength and amplitude when deflected by a material.
  • Raman Spectroscopy: Developed a powerful analytical technique for determining molecular composition and structure based on the Raman Effect.
  • Physics of Musical Instruments: Conducted extensive research on the acoustics of various musical instruments.
